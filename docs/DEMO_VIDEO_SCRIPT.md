## 🎨 Visual Design Guidelines

### Color Scheme
- **Primary:** Purple gradient (#8B5CF6 to #6366F1)
- **Karaoke:** Blue (#3B82F6)
- **Sing-along:** Purple (#A855F7)
- **Success:** Green (#10B981)
- **Background:** Dark (#1F2937) for console

### Typography
- **Title Font:** Bold, modern sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Poppins)
- **Body Text:** Clean, readable (e.g., Inter, Roboto)
- **Code/Console:** Monospace (e.g., Fira Code, Monaco)

### Transitions
- **Between Scenes:** Smooth fade or slide (0.5-0.8 seconds)
- **Highlights:** Subtle glow or pulse effect
- **Checkmarks:** Pop-in animation with slight bounce

### Text Overlays
- **Position:** Lower third or upper third
- **Background:** Semi-transparent dark overlay for readability
- **Animation:** Fade in, stay, fade out
- **Duration:** 3-5 seconds per overlay

---

## 🎵 Audio Recommendations

### Background Music
- **Style:** Upbeat, modern, tech-inspired
- **Volume:** Low (20-30% of narration volume)
- **Mood:** Energetic but not distracting
- **Suggested:** Royalty-free corporate/tech music

### Sound Effects (Optional)
- **Checkmark appear:** Soft "ding" or "pop"
- **Button clicks:** Subtle click sound
- **Transitions:** Soft whoosh
- **Keep minimal:** Don't overuse

### Narration
- **Tone:** Friendly, professional, enthusiastic
- **Pace:** Moderate (not too fast)
- **Clarity:** Clear enunciation
- **Energy:** Upbeat and engaging
